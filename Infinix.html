Summary

Techno Spark 5 was released on .... The Spark 5 comes with a slight screen size difference compared to the Spark 4 released in 2019.
It also comes with four (4) cameras on the rear view whiles the Spark 5 Air has three cameras on the rear. The phone has a Finger Print Scan on the rear panel. The phone offers four colour options that is Ice Jadeite, Misty Grey, Spark Orange and Vacation Blue....
The phone comes with 2GB RAM, 32GB inbuilt Storage and 5000mAh battery. Techno Spark 5 runs Android 10 and with HIOS 6.1 on top and also features 6.6-inch HD+ (720 x 1,600 pixels) display. The Spark 5 measures (174.64 x 79.27 x 9.05mm) size. The quad rear cameras include 13-megapixles main camera with f/1.8 aperture, 2-megapixels camera for closeup shots and the fourth AI camera. The phone also comes with a front camera of 8-megapixels with a Dual-LED flash.
I highly recommend Techno Spark 5 for all.
