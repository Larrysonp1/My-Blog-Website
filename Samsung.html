Summary

Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra 5G smartphone was launched on 11th February 2020. This Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra 5G supports Wireless Charging and as well as Cord/Normal Charging. The phone features an IP68 rating for Dust and Water protection. The phone also supports FaceLock/FaceUnlock properties. The phone was launched in two color options ie. Cosmic Black and Cosmic Grey color options....
It bears a glass body and has 128GB inbuilt storage that can be expanded via MicroSD card ( Up to 1000GB). Samsung Galaxy S20 measures 166.90x76.00x8.80mm (Height x Width x Thickness) and Weighs 222.00 grams. Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra 5G is powered by a 2GHz Octa-Core Samsung Exynos 990 processor that features 2 Cores at 2.73GHz, 2 Cores at 2.5GHz and 4 Cores at 2GHz. It also comes with a 12GB of RAM. Samsung Galaxy S20 is a Dual-SIM (GSM and GSM) smartphone that accepts Nano-SIM and Nano-SIM cards. The phone also has a 6.90-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 1440x3200 pixels and an aspect ratio of 20.9.Samsung Galaxy S20 Ultra 5G runs Android 10, also runs One UI 2.0 based on Android 10 and is powered by a 5000mAh non-removable battery.
Talking about the Camera; It has four back/rear cameras which indicates a 108-megapixels primary camera with an f/1.8 aperture, a second 48-megapixels camera with an f/3.5 aperture, a third 12-megapixels camera with an f/2.2 aperture and a fourth depth camera. Both the rear/back and Front camera also features autofocus.
Talking about the phone's connectivity; it is superb. The connectivity options on the Samsung Galaxy S20 includes Wi-Fi 802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/Yes, GPS, Bluetooth v5.00, NFC, USB Type-C, Wi-Fi Direct, 3G and 4G. Also, the Sensors on the phone include Accelerometer, Ambient Light Sensor, Barometer, Compass/Magnetometer, Gyroscope, In-display Fingerprint Sensor, Proximity Sensor, Fingerprint Sensor.
I highly recommend this smart phone for all.
