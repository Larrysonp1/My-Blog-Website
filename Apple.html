Summary

Iphone 11 Pro Max was released on 20th September 2019. The phone has been the best upgrade of the iphone XS Max but possesses some significant changes for use.
One exciting features is the three camera setup on the rear view which allows you to choose between Wide, Ultra Wide and Telephoto lenses for a perfect view and the phone is (Splash, Water and Dust) resistant...
All these features is backed by the improved HDR and a perfect brand new Night Mode which can be used to make quality images and videos under any condition at anytime. The phone comes with a longlasting battery life that can last for a day or two.
Businessmen should not have doubts about battery running down early but with any workload the phone has the capability to last for approximately two days. The new IOS 13 brings a host of new features and the promise of regular software updates over the period.
I highly recommend Iphone 11 Pro Max for all.
